This game is called air hockey. The goal of this game is to score on the enemies side of the goal and deflect all shots made at your goal. The game works by taking an ellipse and making it so that when it touches certain position it is able to change positions like a ball/puck. When the puck reaches a certain positions i.e like a goal that person will obtain a point. To control your character use the left and right arrow keys on your keyboard
